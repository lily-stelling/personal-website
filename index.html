I’m Lily. I work on AI governance and policy at the European AI Office, where I focus on the safety and security of general-purpose AI models.

From autumn 2026 I’ll be at the University of Oxford as a Rhodes Scholar, reading for a DPhil in Engineering Science on the evaluation of frontier AI systems.

Before Brussels, I studied computer science at the University of Copenhagen and co-founded Make It 16, a youth voting-rights campaign in New Zealand. I grew up between the Netherlands and New Zealand.
