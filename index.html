My name is Lily. I’m a Technical Officer in the AI Safety Unit of the European AI Office. Our unit enforces the General-Purpose AI Code of Practice (Safety and Security Chapter). I’m fortunate to be a Rhodes Scholar, where I’ll pursue a DPhil as an affiliate with the Oxford Martin AI Governance Initiative.

Previously, I co-authored Europe 2031, and rated AI companies’ risk management practices.

I think AI automating scientific and technological research would be one of the biggest events in human history. Most of my work follows from taking this possibility seriously.

Before Brussels, I studied mathematics, economics and philosophy in New Zealand and Australia.
